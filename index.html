TeLetterBot
Just accelerates emails to the Telegram speed

TeLetter is a simple and free Telegram robot who can transform your emails into Telegraph articles and send result to your chat. So magical. Much fast. Wow.


Open @TeLetterBot Get support

Super easy setup

Just open @TeLetterBot or add it to your group/channel
Get unique email address, wich is special for connected chat
Send simple mail to this address from any client
Bot make new Telegraph article and send link into the chat

Flexible

Use this bot as you want: notify your company chat when traveling, build your own mass media, make newsletters for your group and even more. Don't be afraid of experiments!

Open source

Written on Go, based on two fast and simple packages. You can make PR's, open issues and (if you are paranoid) even run it on your own server!
