TeLetterBot
Just accelerates emails to the Telegram speed

TeLetterBot is a simple and free Telegram robot who can transform your emails into Telegraph articles and send result to your chat. So magical. Much fast. Wow.


Open @TeLetterBot Get support

Super easy setup

Just open @TeLetterBot or add it to your group/channel
Get unique email address, wich is special for connected chat
Send simple mail to this address from any client
Bot make new Telegraph article and send link into the chat
